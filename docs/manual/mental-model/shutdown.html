Shutdown

Graceful shutdown is coordinated at the end of a tick. The app declares the policy up front and may supply one no-argument shutdown handler.

Policy Choices

PolicyTrigger checked at tick end
on-last-window-closeAt least one window existed and now no windows are live.
on-window-closeA declared shutdown window kind existed and now no windows of that kind are live.
explicitrequest_shutdown(reason=None) has been called.

Sequence

maybe_begin_shutdown() detects policy satisfaction.
begin_shutdown() marks is_shutting_down.
The optional shutdown handler is called once.
Tick loop stops and root mainloop is asked to quit.
is_running becomes false.
shutdown() is the hard cleanup API. It also calls the shutdown handler once, then destroys windows and root.
